## 📖 About

**Open-Paper** is a growing, community-driven collection of high-quality, clean wallpapers. Every wallpaper is thoughtfully organized to help you find the perfect aesthetic for your desktop, laptop, or mobile device.

✨ **Why Open-Paper?**
- 🧼 **Clean & Minimal** – No clutter, just beautiful backgrounds
- 🎨 **Organized** – Sorted by color and style for easy browsing
- 🌍 **Open Source** – Free for everyone, forever
- 🤝 **Community Driven** – Contributions are welcome!
